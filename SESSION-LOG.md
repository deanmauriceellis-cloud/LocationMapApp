## Session 308: 2026-05-30 — BlueStacks 3D-tilt magenta corruption: root-caused as a renderer-agnostic virtual-GPU limitation (NOT app-fixable); adaptive-GPU fix reverted

The committed adaptive-GPU fix (`0b81e7e`) was **proven ineffective** (I caught my own premature "fixed" — a load-coincidence on the intermittent corruption) and **reverted**; the orthogonal `markerScale` debug log + the investigation doc (`docs/plans/adaptive-tilt-gpu-fix-2026-05-29.md`) were kept. Real hardware (Pixel/Lenovo, real GPUs) renders tilt correctly. Carries: marker **"effects"** (the S307 next-step, never started — the GPU investigation consumed the session), the BlueStacks/emulator support-posture decision, the still-unrun Lenovo 48° OOM gating test.
