## How to use this packet

1. **Read in order.** T2-01 (IP register) is the priority read — it names the candidate inventions and provides the novelty case for each. Chapters T2-02 through T2-04 are deeper reference and context.
2. **Do not file any patent application based on these documents** without a written instruction from the operator. The operator is not authorizing counsel to file; counsel is authorized to *advise on filing strategy* and to take directed action per an executed engagement letter.
3. **Priority items for counsel consideration:**
   - T2-01 §15 — "Legal Protection Roadmap" — 4 provisional patent filings recommended. Counsel confirmation of the filing priority ordering is the primary ask.
   - T2-02 §4 ("Legal & Regulatory Requirements") and §10 ("Required Legal Documents") — confirm the V1 posture is consistent with §10's list and flag anything missing.
   - T2-03 — review commercial strategy for regulatory exposure beyond what V1 declares.
4. **Handling instructions:**
   - Store digitally in a password-protected or firm-standard secure location.
   - Do not email in cleartext or upload to general file-sharing.
   - If printed, destroy hardcopies after the engagement ends (see NDA §8 — Return or Destruction).
