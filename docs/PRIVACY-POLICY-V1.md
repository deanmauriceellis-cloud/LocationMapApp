## 2. Full Privacy Policy

### 2.1 Who we are and what this policy covers

Katrina's Mystic Visitors Guide ("the app") is a GPS-guided walking-tour application for Salem, Massachusetts, produced by **Destructive AI Gurus, LLC** ("we," "us"). This Privacy Policy explains every category of data the app uses, why, where it is stored, and who can see it.

**This is the V1 commercial release.** The V1 app is fully offline: once installed, it does not require a network connection to function, and it does not transmit your location or any other personal information to any server under our control.

### 2.2 What the app uses on your device

While you are actively using the app, it reads the following from your device, all **on your phone, in-memory or in the app's private storage**, and none of it is transmitted off the device:

- **GPS location** — used to position you on the Salem tour map, determine which point-of-interest narrations to play based on your proximity, and compute walking directions between tour stops. Location accuracy is whatever your device's GPS hardware provides.
- **Tour progress** — which tour stops you have visited and which narrations you have heard. Stored locally so the app can resume where you left off.
- **App settings** — your in-app preferences (mute state, narrator mode, map layer choices). Stored locally.
- **Bundled map tiles and point-of-interest data** — shipped inside the app at install time. Not tied to your identity.

**Nothing in this list leaves your device.** The app does not upload your location, tour progress, or settings to any server. There is no "cloud sync," no user account, and no way for us to see where you have been or what you have done in the app.

### 2.3 What the app does NOT collect

- We do **not** collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account identifier. The app has no user accounts.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics SDKs — no Google Analytics, no Firebase Analytics, no Facebook SDK, no AppsFlyer, no crash-reporting service that includes personal data.
- We do **not** serve advertisements. There is no ad SDK in the app.
- We do **not** collect payment information. The single purchase of the app ($19.99) is handled entirely by the Google Play Store — we receive a payout from Google, not any customer payment details.
- We do **not** sell, rent, share, or license any data to advertisers, data brokers, marketing platforms, or any third party. There is no data to sell — none leaves your phone.
- We do **not** collect information about nearby Bluetooth devices, Wi-Fi networks, or cellular towers. The V1 app does not request or use those permissions.

### 2.4 Why the app works offline and what that means for your privacy

The V1 app is designed to be fully self-contained. All Salem tour content — maps, points of interest, narrations, walking routes, historical figures, and the Witch Trials material — is bundled inside the app at install time. Once you have downloaded and installed the app, you can use the entire Salem tour without a network connection.

This design is intentional. By avoiding any server component in V1, we eliminate the most common sources of privacy risk in mobile apps: behavioral analytics, cloud-synced user profiles, ad targeting, and server-side personal-data storage. What we do not collect cannot be lost, leaked, subpoenaed, or sold.

### 2.5 Payment

The app is a one-time $19.99 purchase through the Google Play Store. Google Play handles all aspects of the purchase, including your payment method, billing address (if applicable), tax calculation, receipts, and refunds. Google's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment information. We receive a payout from Google on a periodic basis — not your payment details.

We do not charge subscriptions. There are no in-app purchases or microtransactions in V1.

### 2.6 Children

The app is rated **Teen (13+)** under the IARC content-rating system because it contains historical content about the 1692 Salem witch trials, which includes references to persecution, imprisonment, and execution of accused witches. The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from users under 13 — consistent with §2.3, we do not knowingly collect information from any user.

Google Play's age-based access controls (Family Link, Google account minimum-age requirements) apply to the purchase and installation of the app.

### 2.7 Your choices

- **You can decline data use entirely by not installing the app.** All V1 data use is on-device and starts when you install.
- **You can stop the app's location use at any time** by revoking the location permission in your device settings (Android → Settings → Apps → Katrina's Mystic Visitors Guide → Permissions → Location). The tour features that depend on GPS will stop working, but the rest of the app will still function.
- **You can uninstall at any time.** Uninstalling removes the app and all local tour progress, settings, and bundled data from your device.
- **You can contact us** at the address in section 2.9 to ask a question about this policy.

### 2.8 Data sovereignty

Because V1 does not transmit any data off your device, there is no server, cloud relay, or database storing information about you. The only data this app produces is the data already on your phone, under your control.

Material changes to this policy (changes that expand what the app collects, how it is used, or who can see it) will be accompanied by an **in-app notice on the next app launch** after the updated policy is active, and the revised policy will be dated at the top of the document.

Because V1 collects nothing off-device, any future change that introduces server-side features or off-device data collection would be a "material change" and would trigger that in-app notice.
