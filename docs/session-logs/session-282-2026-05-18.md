### Operator question: does dwell get overridden so other POIs are skipped?

Investigated `SalemMainActivity.kt:2284-2316` (dwell loop), `NarrationGeofenceManager.kt:589-698` (`checkPosition`), and `SalemMainActivityNarration.kt:307` (ENTRY observer).

Findings reported back:

1. **Dwell mechanism is intact.** It freezes the walker when `Speaking || queueNonEmpty` and exits only when `TTS idle && queue empty`, with a 180 s safety cap. During the dwell, `setManualLocation(point)` re-fires every 1 s, which re-runs `checkPosition()` — so other in-range POIs do get a fresh ENTRY chance every second.

2. **Miss 1 = content-authoring gate, not runtime.** SQL on the asset DB showed `14 Mall Street` had `is_narrated = 0` despite 781 chars of `historical_narration` and 812 of `long_narration` populated. The DAO query at `SalemPoiDao.kt:52` is `WHERE (is_narrated = 1 OR (haunt_sprite_id IS NOT NULL AND haunt_enabled = 1))`, so the POI is excluded from `NarrationGeofenceManager.points` entirely — the scanner cannot fire ENTRY on it. One flag fix.

3. **Miss 2 = real runtime miss, root cause not provable from this log.** Both Bell and Armory are `is_narrated = 1`, both HISTORICAL_BUILDINGS/monuments, ~15 m apart. Bell never appears in any `NARR-QUEUE` or `NarrationMgr` line in the stream. Could be (a) scanner never emitted ENTRY (cooldown collision or sub-step distance > 40 m at the relevant interpolated point) or (b) ENTRY emitted but `enqueueNarration` SKIPped silently at the AudioControl group gate (line 953) or duplicate-in-queue gate (line 994). **I didn't capture NARR-GEO this session** (filter excluded it), so cannot distinguish — flagged as a follow-up.

4. **Latent fragility flagged (not actioned):** `events.collectLatest` at `SalemMainActivityNarration.kt:307` is safe today because `enqueueNarration` is synchronous, but if anyone later makes it suspending, concurrent ENTRY events will start being cancelled mid-handler and dropped silently.
