Zero `PoiDetailSheet` log entries from the new PID. The click never reached the sheet. Two distinct causes:

1. **Map marker tap on highlighted POI was intercepted by the S112 highlight polygon.**
   - `showNarrationHighlight()` in `SalemMainActivityNarration.kt:684-712` draws two `Polygon` overlays at the POI and appends them with `overlays.add(inner)` / `overlays.add(outer)`.

   - Inner polygon has `title = "Now narrating: ${point.name}"` â€” this triggers the default `Polygon.onSingleTapConfirmed()` path which opens the InfoWindow and **consumes** the tap.
   - The marker's `setOnMarkerClickListener` below it **never fires**. That is why the log shows no marker-click trace.

2. **Bottom narration banner had no whole-sheet click handler.**
   - `showNarrationSheet()` in `SalemMainActivityNarration.kt:516-568` wires individual buttons (`btnSkip` / `btnPlayPause` / `btnMoreInfo` / `btnDirections` / `btnGeofence`) but the sheet body itself is dead-clickable. Operator tapped the title/text area â€” nothing happened.
   - "Disappeared" = the narration finishes, the banner rebuilds for the next POI, and by the 2nd or 3rd tap the content has changed under the operator's finger.
