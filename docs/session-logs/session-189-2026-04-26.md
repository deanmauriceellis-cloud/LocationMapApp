Validated as well-formed `OpenDocument Text` by `file(1)`; content.xml is 39.8 KB.

One bug fixed during build: initial helper signature `add_text_style(name, **text_props)` failed when called with `text_props={...}` keyword — that pattern packs the dict back into `**` as `{'text_props': {...}}` and odfpy rejects it. Refactored to take an explicit `text_props=None` dict argument with a `_normalize()` helper that strips underscores from keys (odfpy attribute names are `fontweight`, not `font_weight`). Re-ran cleanly.
