### — POI duplicate audit (pass 2) — address-based

Operator flagged Jaho Coffee Roaster & Wine Bar at Derby Street as still showing duplicates after the name-based pass. Investigation showed three real Jaho locations (57 Loring Ave, 130 Canal St, 197 Derby St) with multi-source duplication — same physical location ingested under multiple slug variants from different sources (BCS, OSM, api_sync, destination_salem).

**Address-based dedup strategy:**
1. Normalize `address` → canonical `<number> <street>` (strip city/state/zip, normalize street-type abbreviations like St↔Street, Ave↔Avenue).
2. Group by canonical address; require at least one shared ≥4-char alphanumeric name token (guards multi-tenant addresses where different businesses legitimately share an address).
3. Stoplist for shared-token check: city names (salem, peabody, beverly, marblehead), street types, generic business words (auto, shop, group, office, etc.). Plus dynamic exclusion of any token that appears in the address_key (e.g. street name).
4. Same-category requirement when both have non-NULL category (kills `Witch City Walking Tours/GHOST_TOUR` ↔ `Witch City Broom Making Workshop/ENTERTAINMENT` and `Salem Witch Village/WITCH_SHOP` ↔ `Lost Library/SHOPPING` false positives).
5. "Min unique tokens < 2" check (kills false positives like `Pedro Beato - TurboTax Expert` ↔ `Natalia Ranciuc TurboTax Expert` where two different agents share a brand).

**Operator rule applied:** rows with a UNIQUE `intel_entity_id` (BCS) must stay even if address-collision says otherwise. BCS records come from Google Places — Google's view that they are distinct businesses overrides our address dedup. Filter: only collapse a loser if its `intel_entity_id IS NULL` OR equals the survivor's `intel_entity_id`.

**Result of filtering:**
- Initial unfiltered: 174 losers
- After stoplist + street-token exclusion: 148
- After same-category requirement: 91
- After min-unique-tokens guard: 82
- After BCS uniqueness rule: **24** (final)

**Execution:** 24 rows soft-deleted, marked `data_source LIKE '%address-dedup-2026-04-13-loser%'`.

**Final Jaho state:** 5 active rows (all BCS-anchored: Loring Ave, Canal St × 2 distinct BCS IDs, Derby St × 2 distinct BCS IDs), 5 soft-deleted (non-BCS slug variants).

**Cumulative pass 1 + pass 2:** 2,190 → 2,080 active POIs (-110 soft-deleted total: 86 name-pass + 24 address-pass).

**Files created:**
