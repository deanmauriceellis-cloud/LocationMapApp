### Step 5 — TTS chunking fix ✓

Initial Speak on Stoughton (14K char bio) errored: `NarrationMgr: TTS ERROR`. Android TTS caps utterances at ~4000 chars. Added a `chunkForTts()` helper — splits on `\n\n+` paragraphs first, falls back to sentence-boundary regex (`[^.!?]+[.!?]+`) if a paragraph still exceeds the limit, hard-splits on 3500-char boundaries as last resort. `TTS_CHUNK_MAX = 3500`.

`startSpeaking()` now loops chunks and calls `speakSheetSection()` per chunk with an indexed label "Stoughton (1/5)", "Stoughton (2/5)" etc. Each call enqueues a new segment with a unique `System.nanoTime()` suffix under the `witchtrials_bio_` tag — so `cancelSegmentsWithTag(TTS_TAG_BIO)` still drains the entire queue on Stop.
