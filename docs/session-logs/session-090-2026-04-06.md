## Ultra-Short Teaser Narrations — Free Tier Ambient Audio

**User direction:** Every POI should narrate something very short (~10 sec TTS), like "Pizza at X" or "Drug store at X". Research-backed, best info possible, but constrained to small audio frame. Full narrations behind paywall.

**Approach:**
1. Promoted current `short_narration` → `long_narration` for 702 POIs where `long_narration` was NULL (preserves existing content as paid tier)
2. Generated ultra-short teasers (target 15-25 words, avg 12) for all 814 POIs
3. Teasers mine business descriptions for best available fact (awards, history, specialty)
4. Type-specific templates for POIs without descriptions (restaurant by cuisine, shop by specialty, etc.)

**Generator script:** `tools/salem-data/generate_teaser_narrations.py`
- Description mining: sentence scoring (years, awards, superlatives, Salem-relevant terms)
- Web junk filtering (phone numbers, emails, CTAs, URLs penalized)
- Name-based inference: cuisine from restaurant names, shop specialty, service type, venue type
- Minimum word enforcement: ~8 word floor with type-specific contextual padding
- Maximum word enforcement: 25 word ceiling with clean sentence/clause truncation
- Anti-redundancy: padding skips if overlapping keywords already in narration

**Results:**
- 814/814 POIs have short_narration (teasers) — **100% coverage**
- 814/814 POIs have long_narration (full) — **100% coverage**
- Word count: avg 12.0, min 6, max 25
- Description-backed: 267 POIs with real business descriptions mined
- Type-inferred: 547 POIs with smart name/type inference
- No mid-sentence cutoffs (enforced at generator level)
