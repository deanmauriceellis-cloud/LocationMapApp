## Iteration 2 — pacing cooldown bypass (intermediate)

After the narration-gate and behind-user fixes deployed, second log (`docs/debug-logs/lenovo-walk-no-dwell-v2-20260424.log`) showed the dwell exiting at 4 s when the operator manually skipped narrations. ## Iteration 3 — TTS-gated dwell (final design for this session)

Operator field test of iteration 2 still showed walker striding through narrations. Log analysis revealed the deeper flaw: even with the narration-gate, the CPA model misses POIs whose closest-point-of-approach occurs on the same step as a *closer* POI's CPA — only the closest CPA wins `bestTrigger`, the farther narrating POI is ignored, walker walks straight through its 60 s narration.

**Final design:** dropped CPA entirely. Dwell is now tied directly to TTS state.

Edit: `SalemMainActivity.kt:1750-1840` — replaced the entire CPA detection block with a per-step TTS check. After every 1-second walk step, if any POI is speaking or `narrationQueue` is non-empty, freeze the walker until `(TTS idle OR speaking newspaper) AND queue empty`. Newspapers (segment.id prefix `newspaper_1692_`) are ambient and never pin the walker. 180 s safety cap retained for stuck TTS.

Removed (no longer needed):
- `narrationPoints` load + Historical Mode filter for dwell candidates
- `dwelledIds`, `lastDwellTime`, `lastDistToPoi`, `CPA_WATCH_M`
- LOOK AT bearing-face logic (`bearingToPoi` / `setMovementBearing` for dwells)
- `WALK_SIM_DWELL_COOLDOWN_MS` rate-limit cooldown
