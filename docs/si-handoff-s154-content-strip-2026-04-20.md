# LocationMapApp → SalemIntelligence Handoff: Content-Strip Policy

**From:** LocationMapApp (LMA) v1.5 — Session 154
**Date:** 2026-04-20
**Target:** SalemIntelligence maintainers
**Scope:** What LMA changed in its consumption of SI content; what it means for SI.

---

## TL;DR

LMA now treats SalemIntelligence-generated narrations and descriptions as **legally sensitive** for commercial POIs and **gates them at render time** unless the merchant has bought in (new `merchant_tier` flag ≥ 1). LMA still **ingests** your full narration output — nothing changes on your side right now. But a large fraction of what you generate is no longer shown to end users of the V1 Play-Store build. Please read §5 (implications) before scheduling new narration passes for commercial categories.

No action is required of SI to support the change. This is an FYI so you can prioritize future generation passes accordingly.

---

## 1. What changed on LMA's side

### 1a. Content-strip render gate (Session 154, 2026-04-20)

A new `PoiContentPolicy.shouldStripContent(poi)` gate runs at every LMA render surface (POI bottom sheet, geofence TTS narration, Find list, Tour detail dialog, ambient hints). When it returns **true**, LMA renders:

- **Full-screen painterly category graphic** (drawn from the existing category art library, not per-POI)
- **POI name**
- **Address** (plain text)
- **Website URL** (plain text, not clickable — V1 is fully offline)
- **Phone** (plain text)
- **Hours** (plain text)

…and nothing else. No `short_narration`, no `long_narration`, no `historical_note`, no `description`, no action buttons.

When the gate returns **false**, LMA renders the full historical sheet exactly as before (your narration text, historical_note, hero, etc.).

### 1b. Geofence TTS narration

For stripped POIs, the geofence announcement becomes a single short line:

```
"You are near {name}, at {address}."
```

The body (your `short_narration` / `long_narration`) is not spoken. The existing `AudioControl.Businesses` toggle still gates whether stripped POIs are announced at all (default OFF per S149).

### 1c. Per-POI hero image prune

2,301 per-POI hero WebP files were removed from the APK bundle for 1,832 commercial POIs (~73 MB):

- `app-salem/src/main/assets/heroes/{poi.id}.webp` (S147 triptych tier)
- `app-salem/src/main/assets/hero/{image_asset}` (S119 cel-shaded tier)

Stripped POIs now use a hash-pinned pick from the existing `poi-icons/{category}/*.png` library (40-256 painterly variants per category × 8 spooky styles). Hash-pinning means a given POI always shows the same variant, but different POIs in the same subcategory look different.

These per-POI heroes were generated by LMA's local `tools/hero-triptych/generate_full.py` pipeline, not by SI — but the legal rationale is the same (per-business AI art likely referenced real storefront photography).

### 1d. Schema (no change)

LMA reused the pre-existing `salem_pois.merchant_tier INT DEFAULT 0` column. No new migrations; your SI export of `poi_export` etc. does not need to change. If SI wants to expose a `merchant_tier` or equivalent in the future it's welcome, but LMA does not currently consume it from SI — the field is set LMA-side when (if) a merchant licenses content.

## 3. What LMA still ingests from SI

LMA's content-ingestion pipeline (via `cache-proxy/scripts/publish-salem-pois.js` and the S152 SI KB re-sync) has **not changed**. The LMA Room DB at ship time still contains:

- `short_narration`, `long_narration`, `historical_note` for all 1,769 narrated POIs (including the 1,500+ commercial ones)
- All SI-exported metadata (subcategory, district, year_established, owners, amenities, specialties, secondary_categories, primary/secondary POI identity data, etc.)
- The `salem_witch_trials_*` tables from your 1692 corpus sync

Data is not deleted; it is **gated at render**. If/when a merchant licenses LMA content, flipping `merchant_tier` to 1 on their POI immediately surfaces whatever SI has generated for that POI — no re-generation needed.

Consequence: SI's narration output for commercial POIs is **bundled-but-dormant** in V1.

## 5. Implications for SalemIntelligence

### 5a. Narration generation priority

For V1 (fully offline Play Store release), re-running narration passes over commercial POIs has **lower immediate value** to LMA end users: the output ships but isn't rendered unless that merchant licenses content. Prioritize:

1. **Highest priority** — the 459 kept POIs (HISTORICAL_BUILDINGS, CIVIC, WORSHIP, PARKS_REC, EDUCATION). These always render.
2. **Operational priority** — POIs known to have a pending `merchant_tier` upgrade (LMA will communicate these per-POI via OMEN when they happen).
3. **Low priority** — bulk regen for generic commercial categories. Still valuable (bundled for future monetization), but not user-visible in V1.

### 5b. Content-rating reminder (NOTE-SI008 per OMEN S023)

Because all content that does ship is now the curated-historical subset, the PG-13 / IARC Teen constraint from OMEN-accepted NOTE-SI008 (2026-04-19) is even more load-bearing. Every historical_note / narration / district narrative you generate for the kept categories must pass PG-13. LMA no longer has a "generic business" layer of content to hide behind, so anything in the 459 kept POIs is immediately user-facing.

### 5c. Verified-fact reliability

The long_narration / historical_note contradictions LMA flagged in [ANOM-001, ANOM-002] on 2026-04-20 (`~/Development/OMEN/reports/locationmapapp/S153-si-anomalies-relay-2026-04-20.md`) apply disproportionately now because those anomalies were in HERITAGE_TRAIL / HISTORICAL_BUILDINGS content — the exact tier LMA still ships to users. Any verified-fact reconciliation that lands in SI flows immediately to users on the next LMA re-sync. Top priority.

### 5d. Future: `merchant_tier` plumbing

If/when SI decides to expose a `merchant_tier` in its own exports, LMA can preserve that through the publish pipeline — no architectural block. Not needed today; flagging for roadmap.

### 5e. Salem 400+ launch reminder

LMA's Play Store deadline is **2026-09-01** (Salem 400+ quadricentennial, captures October 1M+ visitors). SI content quality for the 459 kept POIs is on the critical path for that launch. Anything else ships dormant and can be iterated post-launch.
